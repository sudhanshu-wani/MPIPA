What is Khoj Kar ?

Khoj kar is service initiated by the Indian government. It helps in finding missing people. The government ties up with local services and uses face-recognition technologies to track missing individuals.

Methods

A missing person is an individual whose whereabouts are unknown and whose disappearance is a matter of concern to their family, friends, or authorities. They may have disappeared voluntarily or involuntarily due to various reasons such as abduction, foul play, accidents, mental health issues, or simply getting lost. Finding missing persons often involves collaboration between law enforcement agencies, communities, and sometimes specialized search and rescue teams.
